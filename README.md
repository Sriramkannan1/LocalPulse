## 🌟 Overview

**LocalPulse** is an AI-powered civic engagement platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens, local authorities, and service providers through a centralized issue-reporting and management ecosystem.

The platform enables citizens to report local issues, track their resolution progress, discover community events, and connect with verified service providers. Administrators gain access to powerful dashboards for issue management, event administration, and provider verification.

LocalPulse aims to transform traditional complaint systems into a transparent, efficient, and community-driven digital experience.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many civic issues such as:

* Road damage
* Water leakage
* Garbage accumulation
* Streetlight failures
* Public infrastructure concerns

often remain unresolved due to:

* Lack of transparency
* Fragmented communication
* No centralized reporting system
* Poor citizen engagement
* Absence of real-time tracking

LocalPulse addresses these challenges through an integrated digital platform.
